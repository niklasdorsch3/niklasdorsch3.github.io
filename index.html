Artist Statement

Niklas Dorsch began painting in 2019 after moving to New York, where classes at the New York Studio School deepened his commitment to art.

Using wet-on-wet techniques and a minimal brush palette, Niklas begins with abstract backgrounds and gradually "carves out" imagined forms.

Currently, he is seeking to develop a more intentional dialogue between intuitive mark-making and conceptual framework. This period of artistic inquiry represents a crucial juncture in his practice, where he aims to synthesize his technical foundation with deeper thematic exploration.

Through dedicated time and resources, he envisions his work evolving toward a more cohesive body that examines the psychological landscapes where individual experience meets universal narrative.
